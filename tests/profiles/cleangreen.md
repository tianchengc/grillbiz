# Lean Canvas: CleanGreen Solutions

## 1. Problem
* Traditional cleaning chemicals trigger allergies and asthma.
* Local cleaning services are unreliable and frequently change staff.
* Difficulty scheduling and managing bookings online.
* **Existing Alternatives:** Traditional cleaning companies, individual gig workers (TaskRabbit), doing it yourself.

## 2. Customer Segments
* Health-conscious families and pet owners.
* Small business offices valuing green practices.
* **Early Adopters:** Double-income suburban families with pets or young children who suffer from chemical sensitivities.

## 3. Unique Value Proposition
* Premium green home and office cleaning using certified non-toxic, allergy-safe products with a 100% reliable, vetted employee crew.
* **High-Level Concept:** Uber-like simple scheduling meets 5-star hotel eco-cleaning.

## 4. Solution
* Certified non-toxic, plant-based cleaning solutions.
* Secure online booking, scheduling, and payment platform.
* Vetted, fully insured, and highly trained professional cleaners.

## 5. Channels
* Local SEO (Google Business Profile, Google Maps reviews).
* Nextdoor neighborhood partnerships.
* Local print flyers and community sponsorship.

## 6. Revenue Streams
* Recurring residential cleanings (weekly, bi-weekly, monthly subscription).
* Commercial office cleaning contracts.
* One-time deep cleans (moving in/out).

## 7. Cost Structure
* Eco-friendly cleaning supplies and equipment.
* Staff wages, insurance, and payroll taxes.
* Local marketing and booking system subscription fees.

## 8. Key Metrics
* Customer retention rate.
* Average customer lifetime value (LTV).
* Cleaning crew utilization rate.

## 9. Unfair Advantage
* Exclusive local distribution rights for EPA Safer Choice certified green cleaning products.
* Customized booking and dispatch algorithm optimizing travel routes for cleaning teams, reducing fuel costs by 20%.
